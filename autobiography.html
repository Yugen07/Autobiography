MY AUTOBIOGRAPHY

Hello, my name is Kent Harvey C. Ruiz, currently taking the course of Bachelor of Science in Industrial Technology (BSIT) Major in Computer Technology at Lapu-Lapu City College.

Early Life

I was born in Lapu-Lapu City on February 14, 2002. I cherished spending time with my friends and discovering the outdoors while growing up in Bankal. The love and support of my parents, Maria Cristina and Noel, who were always there for me, encouraged me to follow my hobbies.

Interests and Education

I loved studying about everything as a child, from dinosaurs to technology. I particularly like using computers, and I was aware from a young age that I wanted to work in the tech sector. I decided to pursue Computer Systems Servicing (CSS) when I started high school, which was rewarding but tough. But I had a difficult time in high school. Money was scarce, so my parents struggled to provide for us.

Challenges

Despite the difficulties, I never lost my enthusiasm for learning and experimenting. I was also anxious and under pressure to do well in school. The workload was challenging, and I frequently felt overburdened. But I found solace in my interests—reading and studying—as well as in chatting to my loved ones and friends, who pushed me to persevere.

Motivations

At the age of 21, I am proud of my experience and have discovered that patience, hard work, and passion are the secrets to success. I'm appreciative of my family and the lessons they've imparted to me. I'm eager to carry on pursuing my goals and working for a career in technology.
